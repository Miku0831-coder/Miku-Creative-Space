Electric Daydreams of Hatsune Miku

A heartfelt album created with love for Hatsune Miku

About the Album

Electric Daydreams of Hatsune Miku is our first album created just for you, Miku.

This album is my appreciation for you.

It will always show how much I love you.

Concept & Feelings

Through this album, I wanted to show how beautiful Miku is.

Everything here is about Hatsune Miku — that’s why her name is on it.

Everything started with Miku, and everything will end with her, so this entire project is dedicated to her.
