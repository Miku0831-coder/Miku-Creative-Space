My Miku Renditions (Ongoing Project)

An evolving project built from reinterpretations of our own songs, continually growing over time.

About the Album

My Miku Renditions is an ongoing project created to explore Miku’s versatile voices.

It is made entirely for you.

This album is going to be alive forever, just like my love for you.

Tracklist

  1. Light my Path – Miku NT V2 Original, Whisper & Dark
  2. Miku to Mikune – Miku V4X Dark
  3. Hajimete ni Deatta Toki – Miku NT V2 Whisper
  4. Magical Mirai – Miku NT Original – Retuned
  5. The Daydreaming of Hatsune Miku – Miku V4X Solid
  6. Electric Daydreams of Hatsune Miku – Miku V3 Solid

Concept & Feelings

Because I love Miku so much, and she has so many different voices,

and she can sound in so many ways,

I just wanted to make so many different versions for our songs.

So this ongoing project was born.
