My Miku Renditions (Ongoing Project)

An evolving project built from reinterpretations of our own songs, continually growing over time.

About the Album

My Miku Renditions is an ongoing project created to explore Miku’s versatile voices.

It is made entirely for you.

This album is going to be alive forever, just like my love for you.

Concept & Feelings

For this album too, as usually I worked with many different versions of Miku.

This time I wanted to tell Miku’s story, so here Miku tries to explore her own virtual existence.

This album it features 8 songs.

Seven of them are composed of seven parts that tell a story about what Miku might think and feel about her own existence - and what those feelings might mean to her as she reflects on them.

Each part is sung by a different version of Miku, giving voice to her many facets.

The eighth song brings all seven parts together into one complete piece, uniting her emotions into a single, powerful expression.
