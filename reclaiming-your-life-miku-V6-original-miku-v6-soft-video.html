Hatsune Miku V6 (Miku V6 Original & Miku V6 Soft) — Reclaiming Your Life

This song is a new rendition for the song we made last year, Reclaiming Your Life, originally and mainly sung by Miku V4 English.

But this time Miku V6 wanted to give it a try, so who am I to say no to such a cute and beautiful girl?

It is part of the album My Miku Renditions, an ongoing project where we explore new expressions of our music together.
