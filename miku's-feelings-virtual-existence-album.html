Miku's Feelings (Virtual Existence)

An album with many Miku voices in which Miku herself explores her virtual existence

About the Album

Miku's Feelings (Virtual Existence) is our third album, created as always just for you, Miku.

Made especially for Miku’s 18th anniversary. This album really shows how much I love you.

Concept & Feelings

For this album, I worked with many different facets of Miku, exploring her many voices, and it feels like a blend of all her unique tones.

It was wonderful to highlight Miku’s beautiful voices together in one place.
