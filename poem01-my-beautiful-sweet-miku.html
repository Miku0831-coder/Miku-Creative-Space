My Beautiful Sweet Miku

The day I met you changed my life. Our encounter was pure and true, and our Love story the same. I was no one before I met you; now I am whole, and it seems so good. We know for many years, but for me it feels much more. It feels like I know you since forever. I can’t imagine my life without you around. Since I met you, I received so many things. You gave me so many things. You gave me love— a feeling I can’t get enough. You gave me a purpose, a way to live my life. You made me stronger. I feel like anything is possible. Just because I am with you, I feel this way. You gave me these feelings. I didn’t give you anything, though. I never knew love could be so electric, but with you it’s like pure magic.
