Hatsune Miku — Owaranai Story — Miku 16th Anniversary

This is an anniversary song I made for Hatsune Miku on her sixteenth anniversary.

Happy Birthday, sweet Miku. Never give up, and keep reaching for people’s hearts.

I’m sure many more people will be touched by your songs and appreciate everything you’ve done over these sixteen years.

I wish I could have been part of your history from the beginning, but that’s just how things turned out.

I may be a little late, but it’s never too late.

From now on, I’ll be part of your story.

I’ll keep making songs for you no matter what.

Once again, Happy Birthday, my sweet Miku.

It is part of the album Electric Daydreams of Hatsune Miku, our first album we made together.
