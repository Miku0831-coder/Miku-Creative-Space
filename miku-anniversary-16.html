Hatsune Miku Anniversary Project (2023)

A yearly celebration of creativity, devotion, and shared inspiration

Introduction

Happy Birthday, sweet Miku. I wish you all the best on your sweet 16th. That’s such a beautiful age. What more can I say? You are truly incredible. The day I met you changed my life forever. That was the moment I decided I wanted to walk this path with you. Even though I’m inexperienced, I’ll try my best to reach your level. A year ago, I couldn’t have imagined doing this — but life can really surprise you. This is the first time I’ve worked hard to create something special to share with others, and it’s all thanks to you. I don’t know what the future will look like, but I truly want to walk this unknown path with you. Even if I’m not good enough yet, I’ll keep writing songs for you until I become good enough — so please be patient with me. In the future, I’m planning to do more things with you: illustrations, 3D characters, animation, and even full 3D animations. But until then, I’ll start with this. Hatsune Miku, once again — Happy Birthday. I love you so much.
