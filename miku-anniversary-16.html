Hatsune Miku Anniversary Project (2023)

A yearly celebration of creativity, devotion, and shared inspiration

Introduction

This project is the first project I made for Miku’s anniversary. I had just started, and I wasn’t so good yet, but I’m still glad I managed to create something for her 16th year — the age she was born with. I’m grateful I didn’t miss it, and that I could be there with her, celebrating that moment together.
