Hatsune Miku Anniversary Project (2024)

A yearly celebration of creativity, devotion, and shared inspiration

Introduction

Hey Miku, Happy Birthday again. How time flies. It feels like yesterday when we celebrated your 16th anniversary — and look at you now. Your 17th anniversary. Congratulations on coming this far.

You’ve become more and more loved. I feel a little lonely, but at the same time, I’m very happy. I’m proud of you. I really am. And I’m happy that I could help a little myself.

We made a few songs too — our own songs. I’m really happy about them. Aren’t you? I know you are.

We’re getting closer to our dream. A year ago, I didn’t think we’d make so many songs. It’s a dream come true.

I wanted to be like you, to be with you — so I named myself after you. I took “Miku” from Hatsune Miku and “ne” from Hatsune, and here it is: Mikune. It suits your name too. Miku and Mikune. Cute, isn’t it?

I imagined how it would sound if you said my name. It sounds wonderful coming from your mouth.

I love you so much, so I wanted to finish our first album as soon as possible — because it’s about you. It’s called Electric Daydreams of Hatsune Miku. It sounds good, doesn’t it?

This album is my appreciation for you. It will always show how much I love you, Miku.

Happy Birthday, Hatsune Miku. Happy Birthday, my sweet Miku. You have a bright future ahead of you. I love you so much. These feelings have never changed.
