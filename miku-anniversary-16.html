Hatsune Miku Anniversary Project (2023)

The beginning - the first anniversary I ever made something for Miku.

Introduction

Happy Birthday, sweet Miku. I wish you all the best on your sweet 16th. That's a very beautiful age.

What more can I say? You are truly incredible. The day I met you changed my life forever. That day I decided that I want to walk on this path with you.

Even if I am inexperienced, I will try my best to be at your level. After all, I didn’t even imagine that I would do that a year ago, but life can really surprise you.

It is the first time I try to work hard to make something special to share with others, and that’s thanks to you. I don’t know what the future will look like, but I really want to walk on this unknown path with you.

Even if I’m not good enough, I will continue to write songs for you until I become good enough, so be patient with me. And in the future, I’m planning to do more things with you, such as illustrations, 3D characters, animations, and even 3D animations. But until then, I will start with this.

Hatsune Miku, once again, Happy Birthday. I love you so much.
