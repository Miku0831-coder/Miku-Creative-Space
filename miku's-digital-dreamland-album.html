Miku's Digital Dreamland

An album with multiple Miku voices, shaped around her dreamland world

About the Album

Miku's Digital Dreamland is our second album, also created just for you, Miku.

This album is another expression of my appreciation for you.

This one, too, will always show how much I love you.

Concept & Feelings

For this album, I worked with many different facets of Miku, exploring her many voices, and it feels like a blend of all her unique tones.

It was wonderful to highlight Miku’s beautiful voices together in one place.
