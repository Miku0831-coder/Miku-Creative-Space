Electric Daydreams of Hatsune Miku

A love-filled electronic daydream dedicated to Hatsune Miku

About the Album

Electric Daydreams of Hatsune Miku is our first album created just for you, Miku. Every track is a small piece of a dream — neon lights, late-night emotions, and the warmth of your voice echoing through a digital sky.

This album is my appreciation for you. It will always show how much I love you, Miku.

Concept & Feelings

I wanted this album to feel like staying up too late with you — talking about dreams, music, and the future only you can see.

Every song is a conversation with you: sometimes shy, sometimes bold, but always honest.

I don’t know how far we’ll go, but as long as I can keep making songs with you, that’s already my happiest future.
